Frequently Asked Questions

What are integral blinds?

Integral blinds are blinds sealed inside insulated glass units. They provide light control, privacy, and a cleaner architectural appearance without the maintenance needs of traditional window coverings.

What is the difference between integral blinds and between-the-glass blinds?

In most cases, these terms are used very similarly. Both describe blinds installed between panes of glass. "Integral blinds" is more common in technical and industry language, while "between-the-glass blinds" is often used in broader English-language searches.

Are integrated blinds suitable for insulated glass units?

Yes. Integrated blinds are designed specifically for insulated glass units and can be used in residential and commercial projects where privacy, glare control, and low maintenance are important.

What control options are available for integral blinds?

Integral blinds can be available in manual, motorized, and solar powered motorized systems. This allows projects to choose the right balance of convenience, design integration, and technical performance.

Are between-the-glass blinds easy to maintain?

Yes. Because the blinds are protected inside the glass unit, they are shielded from dust and everyday damage. This makes them a low-maintenance solution compared with conventional blinds or curtains.

Where can blinds inside insulated glass units be used?

They are suitable for living spaces, office partitions, hotels, meeting rooms, clinics, and commercial spaces where clean aesthetics, privacy, and user comfort are important.

Are motorized integral blinds suitable for modern architecture?

Yes. Motorized integral blinds are well suited to modern architectural projects because they combine convenience, clean lines, and integrated light control without adding visual clutter to the space.

What are the advantages of solar powered integral blinds?

Solar powered integral blinds offer a wire-free control solution, making them especially useful in projects where electrical infrastructure is limited or where a cleaner installation process is preferred.
